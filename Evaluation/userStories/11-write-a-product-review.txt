User Story 11: Write a Product Review
Description:
As a customer who has purchased a product, I want to write a review and give a rating so that I can share my experience with other shoppers.
Screen Title: Product Details Page
The form must contain the following fields:
Rating (star rating selector, 1-5 stars) *
Review Title (inputText)
Review Body (textarea) *
Buttons:
SUBMIT REVIEW
When the SUBMIT REVIEW button is clicked, the system validates the input. If valid, the review is submitted for moderation and a confirmation message appears: "Thank you for your review! It will be posted after being reviewed by our team."
Business Rules:
BR01 - Rating and Review Body are mandatory.
Error message: "This field is required!"
BR02 - A user must be logged in and have purchased the item to write a review.
Error message (if not purchased): "You can only review products you have purchased."
BR03 - The Review Body must contain a minimum of 50 characters.
Error message: "Your review must be at least 50 characters long."
