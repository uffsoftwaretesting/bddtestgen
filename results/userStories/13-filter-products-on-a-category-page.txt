User Story 13: Filter Products on a Category Page
Description:
As a customer, I want to filter products by attributes like price, brand, and color so that I can narrow down the product list to my preferences.
Screen Title: Product Category Page
The filtering sidebar must contain the following options:
Price Range (slider or min/max inputFields)
Brand (checkbox list)
Color (color swatch checkboxes)
Buttons:
APPLY FILTERS
When the APPLY FILTERS button is clicked, the product grid on the page refreshes to show only the products that match the selected criteria.
Business Rules:
BR01 - The minimum price value in the range filter cannot be greater than the maximum price value.
Error message: "Minimum price cannot be higher than maximum price."
BR02 - If no products match the selected filters, a message is displayed.
Message: "No products match your filter criteria. Try removing some filters."
BR03 - Selected filters should remain active and visible to the user.
On-screen text (e.g., above the product grid): "Active Filters: [Brand], [Color]."
