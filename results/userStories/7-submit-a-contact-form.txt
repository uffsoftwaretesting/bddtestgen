User Story 7: Submit a Contact Form
Description:
As a website visitor, I want to submit a contact form so that I can ask a question or send feedback.
Screen Title: Contact Us
The form must contain the following fields:
Your Name (inputText) *
Your Email (inputText) *
Subject (inputText) *
Message (textarea) *
Buttons:
SUBMIT
When the SUBMIT button is clicked, the system validates the data. If the form is valid, an email is sent to the support team, and the user sees a confirmation message on the screen: "Thank you for contacting us! We will get back to you shortly."
Business Rules:
BR01 - All fields are mandatory.
Error message: "This field is required!"
BR02 - The Email field must contain a valid email address.
Error message: "Please enter a valid email address."
BR03 - The Message field must contain at least 20 characters.
Error message: "Message must be at least 20 characters long."
